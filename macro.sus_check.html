#define sus_check(...)

Verifies that the input, evaluated to a bool, is true. Otherwise, it will panic, printing a message and terminating the program.

See sus_check_with_message to add a message to the display of the panic.

The displayed output can be controlled by overriding the behaviour of sus_panic as described there.
