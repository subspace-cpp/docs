Used to construct a Vec<T> with the parameters as its values.

Calling vec() produces a hint to make a Vec<T> but does not actually construct Vec<T>, as the type T is not known here. The Vec<T> will be constructed once the T is deduced by converting the marker to the Vec<T> type.

Passing no arguments will produce an empty Vec<T>.
