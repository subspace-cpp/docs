Satisfies the Shl<> concept.

This operation supports shifting with primitive signed or unsigned integers that convert to u32, as well as enums. However enum class is excluded as they require an explicit conversion to a u32. Thus the bound is std::convertible_to (implicit conversion) instead of sus::construct::From (explicit conversion).
